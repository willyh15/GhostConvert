About GhostConvert

GhostConvert is built to do common file tasks quickly, without accounts, upsells, or confusing “download” traps.

What GhostConvert is

GhostConvert is a simple toolbox for everyday conversion jobs: turning images into shareable formats, exporting crisp PNGs from SVG logos, handling PDFs (merge, split, compress, rotate, delete pages, protect/unlock), converting documents (PDF ↔ Word, Word/Excel → PDF), extracting audio (MP4 → MP3, M4A → MP3, MP3 ↔ WAV), and unpacking ZIP archives.

Why it exists

Many “free converter” sites are hard to trust: they push signups, bury downloads behind popups, or reuse the same thin template across hundreds of pages. GhostConvert focuses on clear tools, clear explanations, and guides written for real-world use: students, small businesses, creators, and anyone who just needs a file converted quickly.

Files and privacy

Files are handled only to perform the conversion you request. Uploads are stored temporarily during processing and cleaned up automatically after a short period. Details are documented in the Privacy Policy.

How the site is funded

GhostConvert is supported by advertising so the core tools can remain free. If you have feedback, bug reports, or conversion requests, use the Contact page.

GhostConvert is a fast toolbox with no login and no watermarks.
