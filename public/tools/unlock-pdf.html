Unlock PDFs you already own

Some PDFs are created with owner restrictions that block printing or copying text. This unlock tool is designed for documents you already own or have explicit permission to modify, such as invoices, reports, contracts, or training documents.

GhostConvert creates a new, unlocked copy of the PDF so you can print it, highlight passages, or copy text into another document without the original restrictions.

  • Unlock PDFs you created but no longer remember the original settings for.
  • Make it easier to print multi-page reports and slide decks.
  • Copy text from training materials into your own notes.
  • Remove owner restrictions on files you have rights to edit.

It is not intended for bypassing passwords or accessing documents you are not allowed to open in the first place.

How to unlock a PDF

  1. Upload a restricted PDF from your computer, phone, or tablet.
  2. Click Unlock PDF to start the process.
  3. GhostConvert removes owner restrictions and creates a fresh copy.
  4. Download your unlocked PDF and use it like any normal document.

Files are processed for the sole purpose of unlocking and are deleted automatically after a short period as described in the privacy policy.

Responsible use

  • Only unlock PDFs that you created or have permission to modify.
  • Do not use this tool to break into confidential or private documents.
  • Check any agreements or policies that apply to the files you’re working with.

Frequently asked questions

Does this remove passwords?

This tool focuses on owner restrictions such as printing and copying locks. It is not designed for cracking open password-protected files that you cannot open.

Is my unlocked PDF stored permanently?

No. Your files are kept only long enough to perform the unlock and provide your download link, then removed automatically according to the Privacy Policy.

Files are processed securely and deleted automatically after a short period. GhostConvert is a fast, no login toolset designed to stay simple and useful.
