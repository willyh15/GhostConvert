Why convert Word to PDF?

PDF is the safest way to share documents when you want the layout to stay exactly the same on every device. Fonts, spacing, and images are preserved so your resume, contract, or report looks professional whether it is opened on a phone, tablet, or laptop.

Converting Word to PDF with GhostConvert is useful when you:

  • Send resumes or job applications where formatting must stay intact.
  • Share contracts, invoices, or proposals that should not be edited by others.
  • Submit school assignments or essays in a fixed, printable format.
  • Archive important documents in a format that looks the same everywhere.

Your converted PDFs are ready to email, upload, or print without needing any extra formatting work after the conversion.

How Word → PDF conversion works

  1. Upload a DOC or DOCX file from your computer, phone, or tablet.
  2. GhostConvert processes the document and converts it into a PDF.
  3. The layout, fonts, and images are preserved in a new PDF document.
  4. You download a clean PDF that’s ready to share or print.

The converter is designed for simple, everyday jobs: resumes, cover letters, reports, contracts, homework, and any document you want to freeze into a final format.

Tips for best results

  • Check your page breaks and headings in Word before converting.
  • Use standard fonts to ensure the PDF looks the same across all devices.
  • Review the final PDF to confirm all pages and images look correct.

Frequently asked questions

Will the PDF keep the same layout as my Word document?

Yes. The Word to PDF converter is designed to preserve your existing layout, including headings, bullet points, images, and spacing, so the PDF matches the original document as closely as possible.

Can I edit the PDF after converting?

PDFs are meant for viewing and sharing rather than editing. If you need to make changes, it is better to edit the original Word file and convert it again. GhostConvert also offers a separate PDF → Word tool for when you need to move content back into an editable format.

How long are my files stored?

Files are kept only long enough to complete the conversion and provide your download link. They are deleted automatically after a short period as described in the Privacy Policy.
