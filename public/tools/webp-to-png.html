Why convert WEBP to PNG?

WEBP is a modern format, but some editors and platforms still don’t fully support it. PNG is widely compatible and keeps sharp edges and transparency, which is perfect for logos, icons, and UI graphics.

  • Use WEBP images in apps that do not support WEBP yet
  • Keep transparent backgrounds for logos and UI assets
  • Prepare images for older CMS platforms or design tools
  • Export a universal file format for sharing and editing

How WEBP → PNG conversion works

  1. Upload a WEBP image from your device.
  2. GhostConvert decodes the WEBP image and preserves transparency if present.
  3. The image is encoded as PNG (lossless) for broad compatibility.
  4. You download a PNG that works in nearly any editor or platform.

Frequently asked questions

Will PNG keep transparency?

Yes. If your WEBP includes transparency, the exported PNG will preserve it.

Will the PNG be larger than the WEBP?

Often, yes. WEBP is typically more space efficient. PNG is used here for compatibility and lossless output.
