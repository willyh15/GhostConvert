Why convert SVG to PNG?

SVG is a vector format that scales perfectly at any size, but not every platform or app supports it well. PNG is a universal raster format that works almost everywhere from social media uploads to slide decks, CMS platforms, ecommerce stores, and basic image viewers.

Converting SVG to PNG with GhostConvert is useful when you:

  • Need a logo or icon to display correctly in older browsers or email clients.
  • Want a quick preview image for a vector file without opening a design tool.
  • Prepare assets for social media, presentations, or thumbnails that expect JPG/PNG.
  • Share brand assets with clients who may not be familiar with SVG files.

The output PNG files keep the look of your vector art while becoming easy to drop into any workflow.

How SVG → PNG conversion works

  1. Upload an SVG logo, icon, or vector graphic from your computer or phone.
  2. GhostConvert renders the SVG at a clear resolution and exports it as a PNG file.
  3. Colors, shapes, and transparency are preserved in the raster image.
  4. You download a ready to use PNG for your website, app, or document.

This tool is designed to handle simple SVG icons as well as more detailed illustrations, keeping the process as fast as possible.

Tips for best results

  • Start from a clean, high-quality SVG exported from your design tool.
  • Use solid shapes and text converted to outlines for most reliable rendering.
  • For large print work, keep the original SVG as your master and use PNG for digital previews.

Frequently asked questions

Will the PNG keep transparency?

Yes. If your SVG has transparent areas, the resulting PNG will preserve them, which makes it ideal for logos on colored backgrounds or layered designs.

What resolution does the PNG use?

GhostConvert renders the SVG at a high resolution suitable for web and standard screen use. For extremely large print sizes, keep using the original SVG in your design software.
