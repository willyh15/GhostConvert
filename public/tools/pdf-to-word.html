Free online PDF to Word converter

This tool converts your PDF into a clean, editable Word (DOCX) document that you can open in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or other editors. It is designed for everyday documents, homework, invoices, e-books, and forms.

GhostConvert keeps layouts as stable as possible while giving you a document you can edit, copy text from, and reformat however you like.

  • Edit old PDFs without re-typing the entire document.

Converted DOCX files have no added watermarks and are ready to share or print.

How PDF → Word conversion works

  1. Upload a PDF from your computer, phone, or tablet.
  2. Click Convert PDF to DOCX to start the conversion.
  3. GhostConvert processes the document and builds a Word compatible file.
  4. Download your DOCX and open it in Word, Google Docs, or another editor.

For simple documents and text heavy PDFs, the output will be very close to the original. Complex layouts with many columns or graphics may need light cleanup after conversion.

Best practices for cleaner conversions

  • Use the highest quality PDF you have, ideally exported from a document editor.
  • For scanned PDFs, results depend on how legible the original scan is.
  • After converting, save a copy of the DOCX under a new name before heavy edits.

Frequently asked questions

Will the layout be perfect?

Most standard documents convert very well, but complex designs with columns, tables, or unusual fonts may need some manual cleanup in Word or Google Docs.
