PDF to Word (DOCX) Guide

Turn a PDF into an editable Word document you can open in Microsoft Word or Google Docs. Use these tips to get cleaner formatting and easier editing.

Why convert PDF to Word?

PDFs are great for sharing, but editing them can be frustrating. Converting to DOCX gives you an editable document so you can fix typos, reuse text, update forms, and reformat content without retyping everything.

  • Edit homework, reports, and proposals that were saved as PDF.
  • Copy text from invoices, manuals, and training documents into your own notes.
  • Update a PDF template by converting, editing in Word, then exporting back to PDF.
  • Make small wording changes before printing or sharing.

How to convert PDF to DOCX with GhostConvert

  1. Open the tool: PDF to Word.
  2. Drop your PDF into the upload area (or click to browse).
  3. Click Convert PDF to DOCX.
  4. Download your DOCX and open it in Word or Google Docs.

What to expect from the layout

Most text based PDFs convert cleanly. Layout issues are more common when the PDF has complex design elements. If your PDF has multiple columns, lots of tables, unusual fonts, or heavy graphics, expect to do a little cleanup after converting.

Tips for cleaner conversions

  • Use the best source PDF you have. Exports from Word or Google Docs usually convert better than screenshots.
  • If the PDF is a scan, make sure pages are straight and text is readable. Cleaner scans convert better.
  • After conversion, save a copy of the DOCX under a new name before doing major edits.
  • If you only need text, use Word or Google Docs to quickly clean spacing and remove extra line breaks.

FAQ

Will the layout be perfect?

Many everyday PDFs convert very well. Complex layouts may need light cleanup in Word or Google Docs.

Does this work for scanned PDFs?

It can, but results depend on scan clarity. Sharp, straight scans with readable text produce better DOCX output.

Are there watermarks?

No. GhostConvert does not add watermarks to your DOCX output.

How long are files stored?

Files are stored only long enough to complete the conversion and briefly provide a download link. They are deleted automatically after a short period as described in the Privacy Policy.
