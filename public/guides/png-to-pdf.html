PNG to PDF Guide

Turn one or more PNG images into a clean PDF that is easy to upload, print, and share.

PNG is great for screenshots and clean image scans. PDF is better when you need a single document that opens consistently on any device and is easy to upload to forms, portals, and email.

Ready to convert now? Use the PNG to PDF Converter.

When PNG to PDF is useful

  • Homework and assignments that require a single PDF upload
  • Receipts, invoices, and screenshots bundled into one document
  • Scanned pages exported as individual images
  • Forms and checklists you want to print

When you might keep PNG instead

  • Single images meant for a website or social post
  • Graphics that need transparency
  • Images you plan to edit heavily in an image editor

Step by step conversion

  1. Open the PNG to PDF tool.
  2. Drop one or more PNG files into the upload area, or click to browse.
  3. Upload pages in the order you want them to appear in the PDF.
  4. Click Convert to PDF.
  5. Download your PDF and upload, print, or share it.

How page order works

The PDF page order follows your upload order. If you have pages named like page-1.png, page-2.png, page-3.png, select them in that same order so the PDF reads correctly.

If your pages get out of order, rename the files and re upload in the right sequence.

Tips for clean PDFs

  • Use clear, well lit scans so text is readable
  • Crop extra background around your page before converting when possible
  • Keep your PNG resolution consistent across pages for a uniform look
  • If your PDF is too large for email, try Compress PDF after converting

Related tools

  • JPG to PDF if your images are JPG
  • Compress PDF to reduce the final PDF size
  • Merge PDF if you already have multiple PDFs to combine

Frequently asked questions

Can I convert multiple PNG files into one PDF?

Yes. Upload multiple PNG images and the tool creates a single PDF with one page per image.

Will the PDF print correctly?

In most cases, yes. PDF is a stable format for printing. If your PNGs are screenshots, consider cropping them so the content fills the page better.

How long are my files stored?

Files are stored only long enough to complete the conversion and provide your download link. They are deleted automatically after a short period as described in the Privacy Policy.
