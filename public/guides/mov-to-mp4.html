MOV to MP4 Guide

Convert MOV videos from iPhone and cameras into MP4 so they play everywhere and upload cleanly to web platforms.

When to convert MOV to MP4

Convert MOV to MP4 when you need universal compatibility. MP4 is supported by basically every modern browser, most apps, smart TVs, and nearly all upload portals. It’s the best “just works” video format.

How to convert MOV to MP4 with GhostConvert

  1. Open the tool: MOV to MP4.
  2. Drop your MOV file into the upload box (or tap to browse).
  3. Click Convert to MP4.
  4. Download the MP4 and upload/share/play it anywhere.

Tips for best results

  • Use the original file: avoid versions resaved by messaging apps (they often reduce quality).
  • Big file? Shorten or trim in your editor first, then convert for upload.
  • Upload failing? MP4 is accepted by more portals than MOV, but some still have size limits.
  • Web usage: MP4 is the standard for landing pages and embedded video.

FAQ

Will MOV → MP4 reduce video quality?

Sometimes there’s no quality change (container swap). If re-encoding is needed, the output is tuned to keep your video looking clean while keeping file sizes practical for uploads.

Is MP4 smaller than MOV?

Often, yes especially for web delivery. The exact result depends on what codec/settings your MOV uses.

How long are my files stored?

Files are stored only long enough to complete the conversion and briefly provide a download link, then deleted automatically as described in the Privacy Policy.
