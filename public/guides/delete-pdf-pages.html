How to Delete Pages From a PDF

Remove blank pages, cover pages, and sections you do not want to share, then download a clean PDF.

Quick steps

  1. Open the tool: Delete PDF Pages
  2. Upload your PDF
  3. Enter the pages you want removed
  4. Select Delete PDF Pages
  5. Download the cleaned PDF

How to type page selections

You can remove single pages and ranges.

  • Single pages: type numbers separated by commas, like 1,3,9
  • Ranges: type the first page, then the short line key, then the last page, like 7 through 10
  • Mix both: combine pages and ranges with commas, like 2,5 through 8,11

If you are not sure which pages to remove, open the PDF in a viewer first so you can confirm page numbers.

Common reasons to remove pages

  • Remove blank pages from scans
  • Delete cover pages before submitting a form
  • Remove pages with private details before sharing
  • Trim extra appendix pages to reduce file size
  • Keep only signature pages for a quick send

What stays the same

  • The remaining pages keep their original layout
  • Text and images on kept pages remain unchanged
  • Page order of the kept pages stays the same

Common questions

Does this edit my original file

No. A new PDF is created for download. Your original file on your device is not modified.

Can I remove multiple ranges

Yes. You can mix single pages and ranges in one entry.

Are files stored permanently

No. Files are kept only long enough to process and provide a download, then removed after a short period per the privacy policy.
