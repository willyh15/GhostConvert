How to Convert JPG to PDF

Combine photos, scans, receipts, homework, or forms into a single PDF that’s easy to email, print, and upload.

Quick steps

  1. Open the tool: JPG to PDF
  2. Upload one or more JPG files
  3. Select Convert to PDF
  4. Download your single combined PDF

Best use cases

  • Receipts and invoices for accounting
  • Homework and worksheet photo scans
  • Forms and applications that require one PDF upload
  • Multi page “phone scanner” style documents

Tips for cleaner PDFs (if you’re photographing pages)

  • Use bright, even lighting so text stays readable
  • Keep the page flat to avoid curves and shadows
  • Fill the frame with the paper (less background, cleaner page)
  • Upload files in the order you want the pages to appear

Common questions

Can I combine multiple JPGs into one PDF

Yes. Upload multiple JPG images and GhostConvert merges them into a single PDF (one image per page).

Will my original images change

No. Your JPG files stay untouched. The PDF is generated as a new output file.

How are my files handled

Files are stored only long enough to create your PDF and provide a download link, then removed after a short period. See the Privacy Policy for details.
